Nuts & Dried Fruits Packaging

Nature's energy, kept fresh.

The natural oils in nuts oxidize fast, and dried fruits lose their softness in dry air. Our printed rolls and bags shield both, preserving taste, texture and color throughout shelf life.

Why brands choose this packaging

  1. Oxygen barrier: high-barrier laminates stop the oil oxidation that turns nuts rancid.
  2. Moisture balance: keeps nuts crunchy and dried fruits soft — each side of the same challenge.
  3. Light protection: metallized options block light damage to color and flavor.
  4. Strong seals for dense fills: seams engineered for heavy, sharp-edged products.
  5. Appetizing print: warm, photographic designs in up to 8 colors with servo registration.
  6. VFFS-ready rolls: smooth running on vertical filling lines at high speed.

Application areas

  • Nuts: pistachios, almonds, cashews, hazelnuts, walnuts and mixes.
  • Dried fruits: apricots, figs, raisins, dates and prunes.
  • Seeds: sunflower, pumpkin and roasted seed mixes.

Our sustainability commitment

Recyclable mono-material structures are available across this range.

Why Affura Plast?

Based in Gaziantep, a global center of pistachio production, we have deep experience packaging nuts and dried fruits for export markets.

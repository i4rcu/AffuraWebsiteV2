Beverage Packaging

Beverage Labels & Shrink Sleeves

Labels that sell bottles.

On a crowded beverage shelf, the label does the selling. Our PVC shrink sleeves wrap bottles in 360° graphics, and our label rolls carry vivid, scuff-resistant print through icy coolers, wet transport and summer heat.

Why brands choose this packaging

  1. 360° shrink sleeve graphics: PVC sleeves turn the entire bottle surface into brand space.
  2. Precise shrink control: sleeves conform smoothly to complex bottle shapes without distortion.
  3. Vivid 8-color printing: servo-registered flexo keeps brand colors identical batch after batch.
  4. Wet & scuff resistance: labels survive ice buckets, coolers and condensation without fading.
  5. High-speed application: rolls engineered for automatic labeling and sleeving lines.
  6. Light-protection options: opaque and metallized sleeves protect UV-sensitive drinks.

Application areas

  • Water: still and sparkling water label rolls.
  • Juices & soft drinks: shrink sleeves and wrap-around labels.
  • Dairy drinks: ayran, milk drinks and yogurt beverages.

Our sustainability commitment

Sleeve and label materials with improved recyclability are available on request.

Why Affura Plast?

Beverage brands across Europe and the Middle East run our labels — printed on the Comexi F2 MB for color consistency that survives any cold chain.

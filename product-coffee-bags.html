Ready-Made Bags

Coffee Bags

Valve-equipped bags for roasted coffee.

Roasted coffee requires packaging with one-way degassing and a strong oxygen barrier. Our pre-made coffee bags — side gusset, flat bottom and stand-up — combine both with premium finishing options.

Why brands choose this packaging

  1. One-way degassing valves: fresh-roasted CO₂ escapes, oxygen never enters.
  2. Serious oxygen barrier: aroma preserved from roastery to the last cup.
  3. Roaster formats: side gusset, flat bottom and stand-up with zipper or tin-tie.
  4. Specialty-grade finishes: matte, soft-touch and metallic looks that earn their shelf price.
  5. Sizes for every channel: from 100 g retail to 5 kg horeca.
  6. Filling-ready: consistent dimensions for smooth manual or automatic filling.

Application areas

  • Roasted beans: whole bean specialty and commercial roasts.
  • Ground coffee: espresso, filter and Turkish grind.
  • Horeca supply: bulk formats for cafés and restaurants.

Our sustainability commitment

Recyclable mono-material coffee bags are available with valve options.

Why Affura Plast?

We supply roasters across the region and Europe with consistent, filling-ready coffee bags in every format.

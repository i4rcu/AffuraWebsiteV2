Home & Personal Care

Home & Personal Care Packaging

Tough packaging for tough products.

Detergents, surfactants and perfumes place heavy chemical stress on packaging. Our chemical-resistant laminates carry heavy liquid fills, keep powders dry and maintain vivid print through long shelf life.

Why brands choose this packaging

  1. Chemical resistance: laminates engineered to withstand surfactants, enzymes and perfume oils.
  2. Heavy-fill strength: refill doypacks and powder bags that survive 1–5 L and kg fills.
  3. Refill formats: spouted doypacks that use a fraction of the plastic of rigid bottles.
  4. Moisture barrier for powders: keeps detergent powders dry and free-flowing.
  5. Wet-wipe compatibility: films and lidding suited to resealable wet wipe packs.
  6. Category-standard vivid print: the saturated colors home care shelves demand, batch after batch.

Application areas

  • Laundry & dish: powder, liquid and refill detergent formats.
  • Wet wipes: resealable flow packs with label or lid options.
  • Personal care: shampoo, soap and cosmetic sachets.

Our sustainability commitment

Refill pouches replace rigid bottles with up to 80% less plastic — the strongest sustainability story in home care.

Why Affura Plast?

Home care brands need packaging that survives the product inside it. Ours is engineered, printed and tested in Gaziantep for exactly that.

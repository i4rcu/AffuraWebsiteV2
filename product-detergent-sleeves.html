Shrink Sleeve Labels for Detergents

The bottle disappears. The brand remains.

Detergent bottles come in wild shapes — and shoppers see the sleeve, not the bottle. Our chemically resistant shrink sleeves wrap those curves in unbroken 360° branding that stays vivid next to the harshest contents.

Why brands choose this packaging

  1. Chemical-resistant construction: sleeves unaffected by detergent contact and fumes.
  2. Complex-shape shrinkage: smooth conformity on grips, curves and triggers.
  3. 360° category impact: full-surface branding in the loudest aisle.
  4. Scuff and moisture proof: survives wet bathrooms and utility rooms.
  5. Tamper banding: integrated security for trigger sprays.
  6. High-speed application: reliable performance on sleeving lines.

Application areas

  • Trigger sprays: surface and glass cleaner bottles.
  • Detergent bottles: liquid laundry and dish formats.
  • Promotions: duo-packs and bonus-size banding.

Our sustainability commitment

Sleeve materials with improved recyclability are available.

In the cleaning aisle every centimeter shouts — our sleeves make sure yours shouts loudest.

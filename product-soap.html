Soap Packaging

Simple product, refined wrapper.

Soap packaging combines protective function with premium presentation. Our flow-wrap films offer pearl, matte and paper-touch finishes with reliable oil and moisture barriers.

Why brands choose this packaging

  1. Elegant finishes: pearl, matte and paper-touch textures for natural positioning.
  2. Oil & fragrance barrier: keeps essential oils in and moisture out.
  3. Flow-wrap efficiency: high-speed wrapping in the classic pillow format.
  4. Multipack banding: duo and trio banding in the same material family.
  5. Botanical print quality: fine line-work and soft palettes, perfectly registered.
  6. Paper-look options: the kraft aesthetic with film performance.

Application areas

  • Beauty bars: cosmetic and glycerin soaps.
  • Natural soaps: olive oil and Aleppo-style soaps.
  • Hotel formats: mini soaps for hospitality.

Our sustainability commitment

Paper-based and mono-material wrap options are available.

Why Affura Plast?

We produce soap wrappers for regional and export brands, including traditional olive oil and Aleppo-style soaps.

Coffee & Tea Packaging

The guardian of aroma.

Coffee and tea are highly sensitive to oxygen, moisture and light. Their packaging must provide superior barrier and aroma protection together with the premium presentation the category requires.

Why brands choose this packaging

  1. Oxygen & moisture barrier: high-barrier laminates preserve freshness, aroma and shelf life to the last cup.
  2. Degassing valve integration: one-way valves release CO₂ from freshly roasted coffee without letting oxygen in.
  3. UV light protection: metallized and opaque structures protect color and volatile aromatics from light.
  4. Premium finishes: matte lacquer, soft-touch and metallic accents for a specialty-grade shelf presence.
  5. Consumer convenience: resealable zippers, tin-ties and single-portion formats on request.
  6. High-speed filling compatibility: runs smoothly on automatic filling machines with consistent seal quality.

Application areas

  • Coffee: whole bean, ground, instant and Turkish coffee — bags, side gusset and stand-up formats.
  • Tea: leaf tea, tea bags and herbal infusions.
  • Single-serve: drip bags, sachets and portion sticks.

Our sustainability commitment

Recyclable mono-material structures and low-impact inks are available for coffee and tea lines.

Why Affura Plast?

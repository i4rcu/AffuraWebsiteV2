Our Story

From Gaziantep to the world

Affura Plast began in 2016 in Gaziantep, Türkiye, and grew by opening its first branch in Syria in 2024 — building on deep experience in European and Arab markets.

Who We Are

A partner, not just a supplier

Today, Affura Plast provides advanced flexible packaging solutions for clients across European and Arab markets, with a constant commitment to quality, innovation and reliability.

Our products are classified around the industries we serve — food, beverage, ready-made bags, home & personal care and pet food — so every client finds the right solution fast.
